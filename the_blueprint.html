Lana Maugé-Tharpe

'The Blueprint' Zine for The British Library

A project in collaboration with artist Amber Akaunu for 'The Beryl Gilroy Archive' exhibited at the British Library. 'The Blueprint' explores the idea that Black women, and their archives, are the blueprint, which Black women in the present, and future, will build on. Copies of the zine were available at The British Library accompanying the Beryl Gilroy Archive.(Words by Amber Akaunu, Design by myself).
